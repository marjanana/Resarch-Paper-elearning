In this course, you will work on a project that will help you learn the step-by-step process of writing an art research paper.Each lesson is supplemented by sample paragraphs, video demos, and quizzes. You will be able to navigate visited slides only.

The course content is divided into three lessons:

Lesson 1: Research & Annotated Bibliography

You will learn to research for peer-reviewed secondary sources and organize them into an annotated bibliography.

Lesson 2: Thesis & Outline.

Using the annotated bibliography developed in the previous lesson, you will learn to outline the structure of your paper and develop a thesis statement.

Lesson 3: Final Draft.

You will compose the final draft that will contain a clear introduction, body, and a conclusion.

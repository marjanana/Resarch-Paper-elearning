The CCNY Writing Center proposes the following structure of a concluding paragraph:


Thesis (rephrased):restate your thesis
Summary of the essay main points:recapitulate your arguments
Impactful ending:prompt your readers to think more about your topic by ending the conclusion with a quote, call to action, or prediction

The CCNY Writing Center proposes the following structure of an introductory paragraph:


Opening Sentence:gives readers a general idea of the paper's subject
Focusing Sentences:provide relevant background information on the subject
Opposing Opinions:based on research, present the main controversy surrounding the subject
Thesis Statement:gives your educated opinion on the subject and outlines the main points used to support that opinion

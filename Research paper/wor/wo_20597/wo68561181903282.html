Each paragraph develops only one topic in the context of the idea stated in the claim! It has the following structure:


Topic Sentence:signals the topic of the paragraph
Evidential Sentences:quote or paraphrase a source that relates to the paragraph’s topic; they MUST be cited
Analytical Sentences:assess the evidence presented above in the context of the thesis
Concluding Sentence:summarizes the point of the paragraph
